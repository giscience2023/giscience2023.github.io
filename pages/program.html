GISscience 2023

Program

The main conference runs from 13th – 15th September 2023, with workshops and a welcome reception on 12th September.

Tuesday 12th September

  • Workshops are held before the main conference
  • The welcome reception will take place on Tuesday evening. It will be located in the Great Hall.

Wednesday 13th September

  • Full day conference program.
  • Evening is free for delegates to explore Leeds.

Thursday 14th September

  • Full day conference program.
  • Conference dinner at the Queens Hotel

Friday 15th September

  • Half-day conference program (finish at lunch time).
